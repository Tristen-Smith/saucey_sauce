About us

We created our BBQ brand in 2020. We came up with our sauces during a pandemic when we could find any BBQ sauce we emjoyed. We combined our thoughts together and made our brand with all of our quarantine time. With the help of Guy Fieri, we were able to create our own brand. Located in Indianola, Iowa, our Saucey Sauce creation was made. After countless tries, we finally were able to create 4 delicious and savory flavors. We have one store opened for business on the square of Indianola. Our customers come from around the midwest to purchase our products. Our main goal here is to make sauces that all of our customers can enjoy and use for many different food options!
